Barangay Health and Demographic Record Management System

About 86ERS and BHW

86ERS is a unified digital platform designed to automate and centralize the health and demographic record management of Barangay 86, Tondo. It bridges the gap between resident information and community health services by providing a secure, real-time database for both staff and health workers. This integration ensures that barangay operations are more organized, transparent, and responsive to the actual needs of the community.
The Barangay Health Workers feature streamlines the monitoring of medicine inventory and resident medication distribution. It includes a maintenance tracker that identifies high-demand medicines, allowing BHWs to make data-driven recommendations for stock replenishment. This system empowers health workers to focus on resident care by providing clear insights into the community's medical needs and automated reporting tools.

Guidelines

Optimize Inventory Control: Frequently monitor the medicine inventory status to manage stock levels effectively and prevent the expiration or shortage of essential medications.
Track Resident Medication Needs: Accurately log every medicine distribution to maintain a clear history of resident maintenance and identify which medications are in high demand.
Support Supply Recommendations: Use the real-time analytics from the maintenance tracker to provide data-driven recommendations for future medical supply procurement and budget requests.
